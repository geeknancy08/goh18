OUR STORY

Impetus Student Society is a student body in JSS Academy of Technical Education, Noida, which uniquely aims at launching a movemnet to turn good students into better professionals, so as to provide the corporate world with quality professionals and hence making a student's induction into the industry much easier.

Alike its motto is its aim: "Bridging the Gap" between the avenues in the industry and an individual. Impetus lays inititaive to introduce today's academician to tomorrows executive.
